About me

I am an aspiring designer as well as an artist, presently developing, exploring and hoping for the great opportunities in this area of technology. Along with boosting my basic understanding and knowledge of composites and design structure, they have helped me build powerful analytics and technical aspects required for design. My main objective is to garner knowledge about my focus areas, especially UI/UX Design, Development and Research. I aim to fortify my existing skills and develop new ones on the way. All I need is an opportunity to pursue my passion.

When I stepped into the world of UI/UX design, it enchanted me right away. Because I am an artist and art lies within me. Colors, effects, sketches, outlines, the entire process are all not astonishing for me. Thus, I am motivated by this collaboration, creative potential of design and the inventive methodology of product development life cycle and deep research using a user-centered design plan.

I believe I have the technical skills to be a professional designer. I want to better understand people and learn the necessary techniques to research how and why humans use technology in the way we do.
